Rules:

  • Hit the Start Button to start the clock
  • Stop the clock between .97 and 1.00. All other times are outs
  • Stopping on .97 is a single and moves all runners one base
  • Stopping on .98 is a double and moves all runners two bases
  • Stopping on .99 is a triple and moves all runners 3 bases
  • Stopping on 1.00 is a homerun and scores all runners
